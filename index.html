"Every frame is a negotiation between light, patience, and the unpredictable wild."

I'm a 19-year-old CSE student at VIT Chennai who spends every break chasing wildlife across India's most iconic reserves. Computer science teaches me systems — the wild teaches me everything else.

Armed with a Nikon Z system, I've stalked tigers through the grasslands of Jim Corbett, watched leopards dissolve into the Aravalli scrub at Sariska, and tracked Bengal tigers across the ancient terrain of Ranthambore.

My photography isn't about perfect shots — it's about honest ones. The half-hidden, the backlit, the fleeting. Conservation starts with people caring, and people care about what they can see.
