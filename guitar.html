Playing guitar
since school.

I picked up the guitar in 8th grade and haven't put it down since. What started as curiosity became a real discipline — working through theory, grades, and a lot of hours with a metronome.

I've cleared Trinity College London Grade 4 and Rockschool Grade 2, two of the most recognised grading bodies in the world. Exams are just one part of it — the rest is covers, jamming, and figuring out what makes a song feel right.

Wildlife photography and guitar feed from the same place: patience, attention, and learning to stay present in the moment.
